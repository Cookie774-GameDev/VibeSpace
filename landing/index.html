03 — Features

Everything, under one roof

Not another chat window. A full workspace where the work — and the context — stays put.

Terminals that don’t forget

Persistent sessions, fullscreen focus mode, and as many panes as your day needs. Run multiple agent CLIs — Claude Code, Codex, anything — side by side without losing state.

Jarvis & Friday

Two voice presets, your pick of engines — cloud voices through Deepgram and OpenAI, or the free local Kokoro engine that never leaves your machine.

Jarvis calls your phone

Morning briefings, build-finished pings, “call me when it’s deployed.” Real outbound calls and two-way texting, anywhere you are.

112 verified plugins

GitHub, Stripe, Supabase, browsers, calendars — a vetted catalog that snaps into your workspace in one click.

One persistent memory

Chats, agents, tasks, and calls all share a living context map. Close the app on Tuesday, pick up the thread on Friday.

Private by design

Bring your own keys to 21+ model providers, or stay fully local with Ollama and Kokoro. Your chats, memory, and settings live on your device — cloud features are opt-in, never required.
